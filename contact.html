About Us

My name is Daniel Lopez, and growing up in the vibrant yet challenging streets of Chicago, I navigated through my fair share of close encounters with gangs. However, amidst the chaos of the city, I found solace within the walls of my home. It was here that I discovered my true passion – gaming. At a young age, I fell head over heels for the world of video games. My initiation into this digital realm was marked by playing my first game from my brother's collection, a gem called "Getting Up" on the Xbox original. Little did I know, this seemingly simple act would spark a lifelong love affair with gaming. Getting Up wasn't just a game; it was a revelation. It showed me that video games were more than just a source of entertainment – they were a canvas for rich and immersive storytelling. As I delved into the narrative of the game, I realized the power of this medium to convey meaningful stories and evoke genuine emotions. From that moment on, my gaming journey became a parallel narrative to my real-life experiences. While the streets outside echoed with the sounds of urban struggles, inside, I was embarking on epic quests, solving intricate puzzles, and immersing myself in virtual worlds that transported me far beyond the confines of my neighborhood. Gaming became more than a hobby; it became a refuge, a place where I could explore my creativity and challenge my intellect. As I honed my skills, I discovered the intricate artistry behind game design and storytelling, inspiring me to dream of a future where I could contribute to this dynamic industry. In the midst of adversity, gaming became my ally, shaping my perspective and providing an escape from the harsh realities of life. It taught me resilience, problem-solving, and the importance of narrative in any form of art. Looking back, I see the profound impact that a simple Xbox game had on my life. It not only fueled my passion for gaming but also became a driving force behind my aspirations. Today, as I reflect on those early days in Chicago, I recognize the transformative power of video games – a power that turned a young boy's escape into a lifelong pursuit of creativity and storytelling.
